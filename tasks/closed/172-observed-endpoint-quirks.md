## Goal

Record observed endpoint behavior in a durable repo doc.

## Scope

- Write down actual observed quirks from live probes
- Separate observed facts from guesses and mitigations
- Keep the notes easy to update as probes expand

## Behavior

- The repo contains a stable place for endpoint notes
- Behavioral quirks are written down with enough context to reproduce them
- The notes can guide runtime policy and prompt design

## Rules

- Distinguish observed behavior from inference
- Prefer concrete scenarios and outcomes over general impressions
- Keep the notes focused on agentically relevant quirks

## Non-Goals

- No long benchmark report
- No vendor-agnostic whitepaper

## Done When

- Initial live observations are documented
- The doc can accumulate new probe results over time
- Readers can tell what the endpoint currently seems to do under TOAS-relevant prompts
